# USP Document
## Unique Selling Propositions — BTbiz Pharmacy AI Assistant

**Product:** BTBIZ Pharmacy AI Assistant  
**Partners:** BTbiz Insurance | Advisory × Ayudha Pharmacy  
**Tagline:** *Stronger Together · Growing Together*

---

## 1. One-Line Pitch

**The only WhatsApp-native pharmacy platform where patients never download an app — prescriptions, orders, payments, and pharmacist support happen entirely inside WhatsApp, while your team runs everything from one smart dashboard.**

---

## 2. Core USPs

### USP 1 — Zero app install for patients
| Traditional | BTbiz |
|-------------|-------|
| Patient must download pharmacy app | Patient uses **existing WhatsApp** |
| Login, OTP, password friction | Just say **Hi** |
| Low adoption in tier-2/3 | **100% smartphone penetration** via WhatsApp |

**Value:** Higher patient engagement, especially for Ayudha Pharmacy's existing customer base.

---

### USP 2 — End-to-end order lifecycle on autopilot
- **13 order statuses** from prescription to refill reminder
- Automatic WhatsApp notification at every step
- Pharmacist only clicks status — patient is informed instantly

**Value:** Fewer phone calls asking "mera order kahan hai?"

---

### USP 3 — Unified inbox (WhatsApp + dashboard)
- Every patient message, bot reply, and prescription in **one inbox**
- Pharmacist replies from dashboard → delivered on patient's WhatsApp
- Prescription images: view, open in new tab, download

**Value:** No more lost messages on personal staff phones.

---

### USP 4 — Intelligent WhatsApp bot (not just auto-reply)
- Welcome image + branded greeting
- Interactive **service menu** (8 options)
- Medicine stock & price lookup
- FAQ auto-answers
- Repeat last order in one tap
- Store location & hours

**Value:** 24/7 first-line support without extra staff.

---

### USP 5 — Payment collection on WhatsApp
- Payment link + QR code sent automatically on **Payment Pending**
- Configure once in Settings — applies to all orders
- Override per order when needed

**Value:** Faster payment collection; less manual UPI sharing.

---

### USP 6 — BTbiz + Ayudha brand trust
- Custom welcome banner (BTbiz × Ayudha Pharmacy)
- Professional first impression on every new patient chat
- Configurable greeting image per pharmacy

**Value:** Brand consistency across insurance advisory and pharmacy channels.

---

### USP 7 — Multi-tenant SaaS ready
- One platform, multiple pharmacies
- Each pharmacy: own WhatsApp number, patients, settings, catalog
- Isolated data per tenant

**Value:** BTbiz can onboard more partner pharmacies without new codebase.

---

### USP 8 — Meta template ready (enterprise-grade)
- Supports Meta-approved WhatsApp templates for order updates
- Fallback to session messages if template fails
- Ready for 24h+ proactive notifications (refill reminders)

**Value:** Compliant with Meta messaging policies at scale.
